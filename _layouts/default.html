I'm a research assistant at the Computer Vision Department of MBZUAI, UAE. I'm currently working on explainability of multimodal and vision transformers under the supervision of Dr Salman Khan.

I completed my undergrad at the Department of Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering, University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. There I worked on dynamic object detection, tracking and trajectory prediction for autonomous driving, advised by Dr Peshala Jayasekara and Dr Ranga Rodrigo. I completed my internship working on an industrial machine vision project at Creative Software in collaboration with Cognite AS.

I'm interested in Computer Vision and Machine Learning with focus on the subdomains of multi-modal learning, self-supervised learning, and explainability.
